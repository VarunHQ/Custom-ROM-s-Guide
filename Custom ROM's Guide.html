What is a Custom ROM?

A custom ROM is a modified version of the Android operating system that can be installed on your device to enhance performance, add new features, or provide a different user experience.
It can make older phones feel new again with the latest updates and features.

Top Custom ROMs in 2026

  1. LineageOS: Known for its stability and wide device support.
  2. Pixel Experience: Brings the Google Pixel experience to other devices.
  3. Resurrection Remix: Offers a highly customizable user interface.
  4. Evolution X: Known for its smooth performance and regular updates.

Note: Installing a custom ROM may void your warranty and can potentially brick your device if not done correctly. Always follow instructions carefully and back up your data before proceeding.

For more information and guides on how to install custom ROMs, visit the official websites of the respective ROMs or trusted Android forums.
